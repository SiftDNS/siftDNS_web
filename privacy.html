SiftDNS Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 3, 2025

At SiftDNS ("we", "us", "our"), your privacy is the foundation of our service. This policy outlines the extremely limited information we handle and our unwavering commitment to protecting your data.

Important Distinction: Our **DNS Service** (the product you use) is separate from our **Website** (siftdns.com). This policy covers both. The DNS Service has a strict **zero-logging policy**. Our Website uses privacy-respecting analytics to count visitors, which does not track you across the internet.

1. Our Zero-Log DNS Service

Our core promise is simple: we do not log your DNS queries. When you use SiftDNS, we don't know who you are, what your IP address is, or what websites you visit. Your browsing activity is never stored, analyzed, or sold. It's your business, not ours.

To ensure our network runs smoothly, we monitor aggregate, anonymized traffic data (e.g., total queries per server). This data contains no personally identifiable information.

2. What Information We Handle and Why

We've built our systems to function without needing your personal data. The only situations where we encounter any information are:

  • When you pay for SiftDNS: Your payment and subscription are managed entirely by our payment processor, Stripe. We do not see or store your name, credit card details, or billing address. We only receive a basic token from Stripe that confirms you have an active subscription.
  • When you contact us for support: If you email us for help, we will have your email address and the contents of our conversation. We only use this to help you and will delete the conversation upon your request.
  • When you visit our website: We use a privacy-focused analytics tool to count website visitors and see which pages are popular. This helps us improve our site. This tool does not use cookies or track your activity across other sites.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Because we collect almost no data, there is very little to share. The only circumstances in which information might be disclosed are:

  • With Service Providers: We use Stripe for payments and trusted vendors for our server infrastructure. These partners are chosen for their high security and privacy standards.
  • To Comply with the Law: We are based in the United States. If we receive a valid legal order from a court with proper jurisdiction, we would be required to comply. However, since we do not store DNS logs or personally identifiable subscriber information, we would have nothing to provide in response to requests for that data.

4. Data Security and Retention

We employ strong technical measures to protect our infrastructure, including encryption and secure server configurations. As we don't store your personal data, the risk of a breach is minimized by design.

Any support emails we have are retained only as long as needed to assist you and are deleted upon request.

5. Your Data Rights (GDPR, CCPA)

You have rights over your data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since we hold almost no personal information, these rights are straightforward:

  • To manage your subscription and billing information, please use the Stripe customer portal, as they are the controller of that data.
    • - **To access or delete a support conversation**, simply email us from the same address, and we will handle your request promptly.

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will post any updates on this page and notify active subscribers of significant changes via email.
